About Todd DeLuca

Todd DeLuca married Haidee Michie Heyward in 2007. They are avid long-distance hikers and thru-hiked the Appalachian Trail in 2003, the Pacific Crest Trail in 2008, and half of the Continental Divide Trail in 2009. More about that at their website, www.heyluca.com. In 2011, they were joined by their son, John Joseph DeLuca.

Todd grew up in Weston CT, got his B.A. in Computer Science from Brown University in Providence RI, and has lived in both Boston MA and New York City NY. He has worked as a computer scientist at BBN and a software engineer at NewView.com. He currently works as a scientific programmer at the Wall Laboratory in the Center for Biomedical Informatics at Harvard Medical School. His professional experience and interests include statistical data analysis, machine learning, and software engineering.
